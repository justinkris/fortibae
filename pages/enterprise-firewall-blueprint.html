LESSON 10
Hardware Acceleration
0% DONE
  • SPU overview: NP (network) vs CP (content) vs SOC (combined)
  • NP6 — FortiGate 200E, 2000E, 2500E
  • NP7 — FortiGate 400F, 600F, 900G (ports X5–X8)
  • CP9 — content processor, SSL/IPS/AV acceleration
  • SP5 / SOC5 — entry/mid-range combined chip (e.g., FortiGate 91G)
  • CLI: diagnose hardware lspci | grep 1a29 → NP ID (4e36=NP6, 4e37=NP7)
  • CLI: get hardware status → ASIC version (CP9, SOC5)
  • NP Direct architecture — reduces latency, same-NP port requirement
  • Traffic offload conditions — what can and cannot be offloaded

Security Profiles

LESSON 7
IPsec
0% DONE
  • IPsec topologies: site-to-site, hub-spoke, dual hub, full mesh, ADVPN
  • IKEv2 advantages: AES-GCM, EAP auth, stronger DH groups
  • DH groups: MODP (14–18) vs ECP (19–20) — weak = groups 1, 2, 5
  • PFS — Perfect Forward Secrecy in Phase 2
  • DPD on-demand — unpredictable traffic
  • DPD on-idle — regular/periodic traffic
  • DPD disabled — stable networks, only replies to peer DPDs
  • FortiManager IPsec templates — simplify large-scale VPN deployment
